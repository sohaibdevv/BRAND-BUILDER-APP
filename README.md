# 🌟 Brand Builder Studio

Welcome to **Brand Builder Studio** (the Ad-Visualization Laboratory), an elegant full-fidelity conceptual sandbox that structures, plans, and visualizes physical product designs seamlessly across distinct commercial landscapes.

Featuring the **Artistic Flair** visual design theme, this system combines deep charcoal canvases with eye-catching high-reactivity acid-lime accents to present a beautifully responsive, modern workstation.

---

## 🎨 Creative Vibe & Visual Design

Brand Builder is styled under the **Artistic Flair** digital aesthetic:
- **Ultra-Contrast Canvas:** Deep rich blacks and coal-greys (`#0A0A0A`, `#111F1F`) to keep core product mockups framed in beautiful negative space.
- **High-Activity Lime Accents:** Highlighting critical system indicators, status lines, and call-to-actions in vibrant `#C4FF00`.
- **Structured Monospace:** Employs precise display typography combined with monospace technical readouts to give an authentic studio laboratory feel.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Unified Brand Blueprint Synthesis
Input standard physical descriptions (textures, closures, materials, stamping details) and custom vibes. The laboratory dynamically outputs:
- A memorable **Trade Brand Name** (if none is supplied).
- An evocative **Slogan** aligning with your target design aesthetic.
- A highly descriptive **Consistent Product Style Guide Capsule** used dynamically during generation passes.

### 2. Multi-Medium Campaign Generator
Render custom advertisement mockups with synchronized product features across three distinct proportions:
-   **Medium 01 / Outdoor Billboard (16:9):** Displays massive high-contrast city highway billboard frameworks mimicking real physical support structures. Implements custom landscape configurations.
-   **Medium 02 / Social Media Feed (1:1):** Embeds studio placement shots inside a fully rendered dark-mode social media mobile post frame complete with sponsored tags, likes, and dynamic brand captions.
-   **Medium 03 / Newspaper Column (3:4):** Renders aged editorial paper mockups incorporating double-ruled traditional borders, historical headers, and rasterized monochrome portrait layout illustrations.

### 3. Safety Guarantees & Active Inspection
- **Strict Anti-Human Safeguard:** Under-the-hood parameters actively restrict rendering engines from deploying passengers, faces, hands, or viewers to ensure shots remain purely product-focused.
- **Interactive Inspector Modal:** Examine any rendered asset in full-fidelity. Read the exact model payload, copy raw structured prompts instantly, or export transparent files to your computer.

## 🛠️ How to Use

Follow these simple steps to run your first advertising campaigns:

1. **Set Up Keys:** Ensure your `GEMINI_API_KEY` is configured in the environment settings dashboard.
2. **Select or Describe:** Enter detailed physical descriptors in the main workstation editor (or select one of the high-fidelity preconfigured presets like **Zest Ceramic Seltzer** or **Era Wellness Cylinder**).
3. **Draft the Blueprint:** Press the **Initiate Brand Blueprint** command. The studio directors will synthesize trade labels and construct custom prompt templates.
4. **Trigger Mockups:** Fire visual generation queries on one or all media templates (Billboard, Social, Newspaper) to fetch beautiful, high-fidelity AI-generated imagery.
5. **Inspect & Tweak:** Toggle custom prompt strings directly within individual containers to test alternative environments, then inspect and download your results!

## 💻 Tech Stack & Architecture

- **Engine Workframe:** React 18+ with Vite (Single Page Application structure).
- **Core Utility Styling:** Tailwind CSS for layout, custom spacing, typography pairing, and visual borders.
- **Motion Framework:** Staggered transitions and micro-interactions powered by `motion` (Framer Motion).
- **Visual Iconography:** Polished symbols from `lucide-react`.
